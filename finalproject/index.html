How We Stay in Touch with You

Our teachers offer classes both in-person and virtually. We do this to help ensure our students get the help they need. The student will also have access to our virtual classroom in Canvas. This will allow our students the ability to access songs, videos, books, announcements and other assigned material. They will also be able to access their teacher's virutal office on zoom. The student will also be able to contact their teacher via canvas message, email, and the ability to text or call their teacher anytime during the week. Learn more about what we offer at our academy by sending us a message or give us a call!

Concert Fun!

Here at L&C Music Academy we like to have fun! Twice a year during the Spring and Fall our staff will hold some fun concerts for the students to show what they have learned. The concerts are not mandatory but are highly encouraged by our teachers. The details of the concert will be given by the teachers at the appropriate time. Each student will have sufficient time to practice and feel comforatable with each of their songs.
