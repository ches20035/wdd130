My Content

Our Classes

Our academy offers a wide range of classes that are sutiable for all ages. A short synopis is found below for each of the classes that are offered. If you have further questions, please feel free to reach out to us on our contact page.

Guitar Lessons

Our teachers are ready to help you learn the basics of guitar. You will learn some basic and advanced chords that you will incoroporate into songs, this included learning how to read a chord diagram. You will learn the basics of tablature and know how to read it. Later, you will learn the basics of note theory, if you choose to of course! The lessons will also include how to both strum and use the fingerstyle method. You teacher will plan the lesson based on what the student both needs and wants to learn. So come and find out for yourself and register for 1 hour private session today!

Ukulele Lessons

Do you want to learn the ukulele? Are you not sure how to start? Register for a private session with one of our amazing faculty. You will learn some basic and advanced chords that you can use in the songs you choose to learn. You will learn how to read chord diagrams and learn how to both strum and pick.You will also learn how to read and write tablature. If you choose, the teacher will help you learn and understand note theory basics that will help you become a pro ukulele player.

Voice Lessons

Become a pro singer through our voice lessons that we offer here at L&C Music Academy. Your teacher will help you get out of your comfort zone and learn some great voice techniques. You will learn the basics of Tonic Solfedge, which is Do Re Mi Fa So La Ti Do. You wil also learn some breathing techniques and some warm-up exercises that will help you take control of your voice. The student will become familiar with sight singing and will learn many songs of their own choice as well some that the teacher will choose for them. So get registered for your private voice session today!

Piano Lessons

Come and find out why there are so many students that register for a private piano session with L&C Music Academy! The student will learn the basics of note theory. They will become familiar with the keys and where each of the notes are found on the piano. The student will learn about both the treble and bass clefs. The student will learn how to both read and write the notes and will be able to play any song using a song sheet. So come and get started with your private piano session today!

Please Note: The teacher will choose one song to learn, the student will choose two songs to learn, and finally the student will write their own song with the help of their teacher for each lesson.

About Us

David Chesley, the founder of L&C Music Academy began teaching his first lesson in the year of 2015, just after he came home from serving an LDS mission. His first lesson took place in the small town of Wellsville, just south of Logan in the state of Utah. He orginally called the business "David Chesley Music Lessons". The business soon took off, and has since grown considerably. A year later, David met the love of his life, and began dating. A year later, he proposed to her and was married a few months later. It wasn't until then, that the small academy went through some drastic changes. The academy began to offer wide variety of lessons, including: guitar, ukulele, voice, and piano. The academy also offered lessons both in person and a new online option. The name was later changed to L&C Music Academy.

David also offered something that very few teachers would ever consider. He would travel to his students homes. This made his business stand out from the crowd. This allowed some flexibity for his studnets and the parents that were involved. Another unique tool that the academy has to offer is the ability to access the classroom materials, such as songs, lesson plans, video tutorials, and other activities through the Google Classroom platform. The classroom was later changed to what David believed was a better solution, the academy upgraded to the Canvas platform. The platform is very similar to Google classroom but offers more tools within it that would help the studnet and teacher stay connected. So what are you waiting for?
