Site Plan

David Chesley

Site Name

  • L&C Music Academy
Our Purpose
  • Are you interested in learning a new instrument? Do you have a child or another family member that you would like to enroll in a class? I have created a website that will allow you to find your new instrument and begin classes today. It is easy to navigate and find an instructor that will best fit you or your family members needs. You will also be able to access many other resources such as songs and video tutorials.
Our Goal
  • Introduce our business to as many people as possible in order to enroll students into our music classes.
  • Help people find the right instrument for them.
Target Audience
  • A customer is interested in learning a new instrument and wants to know more information about the classes.
  • A customer is referred to one of our teachers by a friend or family member and wants to know more.
  • A customer is trying to find a tutorial on how to tune the guitar and how to play some basic chords.
Color Scheme Typography
  • I will be using the Sans Seriff font.
