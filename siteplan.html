Site Plan

David Chesley

Site Name

  • Chesley Rafting Expeditions
Our Purpose
  • Inform our readers of new and upcoming rafting expeditions within our company.
  • Inform our readers of upcmoing events or any other news from the company.
  • Offer repair services to those customers who need their raft repaired.
Our Goal
  • Invite as many people who are interested to join one of our expeditions and become a member of our community Facebook page.
Target Audience
  • A customer is interested in one of our expeditions and decides to reach out.
  • A customer is interested in becoming a member of our Facebook community and reaches out to one of our staff.
  • A friend referred one of their friends to our company for their rafting repair needs.
  • A customer reach out because one of their friends is trypped in the middle of an enormous river and needs rescued.
Color Scheme Typography
  • I will be using the Google font theme called "Roboto."
